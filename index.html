Top Fishing Locations

Ranked by the balance of safety and gold per hour. All are coastal/saltwater spots where you can park and cast without moving.

The far northeast coastline is one of the most remote places in the game. No nearby Horde flight path. Slightly better Stonescale Eel catch rates. Best during off-peak hours or on low-pop servers.

What to Sell vs. Cook

Your 300 Cooking skill is key to maximizing profit. Some fish are worth more raw, others gain significant value when cooked.

Fish Action Result Why
Stonescale Eel Sell RAW — Alchemists need Stonescale Oil for Flask of the Titans. Raw price beats any cooked value.
Winter Squid Cook → Sell Grilled Squid (+10 Agi) Rogues and hunters buy these constantly for raids and PvP. Cooked value significantly higher.
Raw Glossy Mightfish Cook → Sell Mightfish Steak (+10 Sta) Tanks want these for raid preparation. Cooking adds solid value.
Nightfin Snapper Cook → Sell Nightfin Soup (+8 mp5) Healers and casters love this. Only from freshwater at night.
Lightning Eel Sell RAW — Rare alchemy reagent. Sells well as-is.
Everything else Vendor — Not worth the AH listing fees. Vendor for a few silver each.

Time of Day Strategy

Coastal fish (Stonescale Eel, Winter Squid, Mightfish) are available 24/7 from saltwater. But if you're willing to make one move per session, a night rotation can boost profits.

Daytime — 06:00 – 18:00 Server
Stay coastal at Feathermoon docks

Target: Stonescale Eel, Winter Squid, Mightfish. Your full standard rotation.

Nighttime — 18:00 – 06:00 Server
Option: move to Feralas inland lake

Target: Nightfin Snapper (cook into Nightfin Soup). Popular with healers. Only spawns from freshwater at night.

If you refuse to move at all, stay coastal full-time. The gold/hour is solid around the clock.

Gear & Consumables Checklist

  • Fishing Pole: Big Iron Fishing Pole (+100) or Arcanite Fishing Rod (+35). With 300 base, you won't miss casts, but higher skill slightly boosts rare catch rates.
  • Lure: Aquadynamic Fish Attractor (+100 for 5 min). Buy stacks from engineering suppliers.
  • Cooking Supplies: Carry plenty of Simple Wood and Flint & Tinder to cook on the spot.
  • AH Strategy: List fish in stacks of 5 and 20 for maximum sales. Raid nights (Tues/Wed) see highest demand.
  • Bag Space: Bring empty bags. Fishing generates a lot of items per hour.

Quick Start

Go to Feathermoon Stronghold in Feralas right now. Stand on the dock. Fish into the ocean. Cook Winter Squid into Grilled Squid and Mightfish into Mightfish Steak. Sell Stonescale Eel raw on the AH. List everything in stacks of 5 and 20. Winter Squid season ends around March 20 — you have a few weeks of peak profit left.

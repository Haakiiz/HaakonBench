### Chapter 1: Gear, Setup & Warrior-Specific Tips ("The Armored Angler")
- **Fishing Kit**: 300 skill + lure/pole buffs for minimal "fish get away." Bring multiple poles if enchanted differently.
- **Cooking Synergy**: Always cook what you catch. Nightfin Soup (mana regen) sells for a premium to casters/raiders. Stonescale Eels feed into high-end Alchemy. Other cooks: Savory Deviate Delight (if you snag any), Grilled Squid, or Rockscale Cod dishes.
- **Warrior Perks**: Mount up for quick travel between spots. Use a hearthstone to a major city (Stormwind) or Theramore for safety resets. Defensive cooldowns (Shield Wall, etc.) for rare interruptions. Park in a spot with line-of-sight to guards or water for quick escapes.
- **AFK Features**: Face away from potential paths, use auto-cast macros if possible, and monitor via sound or occasional checks. Bring food/water for long sessions.
- **Bonus**: Fish during peak demand times (pre-raid resets for soups/flasks).

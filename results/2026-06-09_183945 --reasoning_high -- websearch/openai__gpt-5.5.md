# The Gold/Hour Model

Fishing is realm-price sensitive, so here is a practical model:

- Assume **~150 catches/hour** while stationary and paying attention. Legacy-WoW’s Stonescale data uses roughly this assumption and explicitly models “five fish every two minutes,” or **150/hour**. ([legacy-wow.com](https://legacy-wow.com/fishing-guide-catching-stonescale-eels/))
- Faction AH cut is **5%**, so your true sale value is roughly `AH price × 0.95`, before deposits. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/classic-auction-house-economy?utm_source=openai))
- Use this formula:

> **Gold/hour = fish per hour × sale price × 0.95 + bycatch value − vendor-mat cost**

For estimates below, I’ll give a realistic range and then tell you when to scale it up. If your realm has inflated Classic Era prices, especially Stonescale Eel at 2g+, your GPH can be much higher.

# Tier List of Stationary Fishing Spots

## 1. Azshara — Bay of Storms “Hydraxis Hermit” Spot

**Purpose:** Maximum Stonescale Eel / high-level coastal fish  
**Safety:** Medium-low on PvP, good if hidden  
**Movement:** None once positioned  
**Best time:** **00:00–06:00 server time** for Stonescale Eel  
**Current June daytime note:** Winter Squid is unavailable; daytime gives Summer Bass instead.

### Where to stand

Go to **Duke Hydraxis’s island area**, around **79.2, 73.6**, in the Bay of Storms. Duke Hydraxis is located on a remote Bay of Storms island in Classic. ([warcrafttavern.com](https://www.warcrafttavern.com/wow-classic/guides/hydraxian-waterlords-reputation-guide/?utm_source=openai))

Your goal is to stand on a small rock/island edge where:

- Minimap says **Bay of Storms**
- No naga/giant/elemental path reaches you
- Your bobber lands in fishable water
- You are visually annoying to notice from the shore

Wowhead specifically notes that the Bay of Storms coast has dangerous wanderers, but that there are **small unoccupied islands** where you can safely fish. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/best-fishing-pools-farming-locations-classic-wow))

### What you catch

Bay of Storms is the premium high-level water: Darkclaw Lobster, Large Raw Mightfish, seasonal Summer Bass/Winter Squid, Stonescale Eel, and other valuable fish. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/best-fishing-pools-farming-locations-classic-wow))

That implies around **30–35 Stonescale Eels/hour** if you are landing about 150 catches/hour. ([legacy-wow.com](https://legacy-wow.com/fishing-guide-catching-stonescale-eels/))

### Estimated gold/hour

| Server economy | Estimate |
|---|---:|
| Low/fresh prices | **18–35g/hour** |

The big variable is Stonescale Eel. It is valuable because it becomes **Stonescale Oil**, which is used in high-end alchemy, including **Flask of the Titans** and other defensive consumables. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/best-fishing-pools-farming-locations-classic-wow))

- **Large Raw Mightfish → Mightfish Steak** if your tanks buy it.

### Verdict

This is your **highest GPH stationary fishing location**. The only reasons not to do it are PvP risk, getaways from low effective Fishing, or if your realm’s Stonescale price is dead.

# Cooking Profit Rules

## 1. Raw Nightfin Snapper → Nightfin Soup

**Usually cook.**

Nightfin Soup gives **8 MP5 for 10 minutes** in Classic and is valuable to healers/casters. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item%3D13931/nightfin-soup?utm_source=openai))

Sell in:

- Stacks of **5** for casual dungeon/raid prep buyers
- Stacks of **20** for guild raiders
- Post shortly before common raid times

## 2. Winter Squid → Grilled Squid

**Cook if cooked price beats raw + spice.**

Grilled Squid gives **+10 Agility** and is a classic melee/hunter consumable. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item%3D13928/grilled-squid))

Best play:

- Fish heavily in winter.
- Cook half, keep half raw.
- Sell cooked Grilled Squid when Winter Squid is out of season.

## 3. Stonescale Eel

**Usually sell raw.**

It is an alchemy reagent for Stonescale Oil, which feeds important raid consumables like Flask of the Titans and Greater Stoneshield Potion. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/best-fishing-pools-farming-locations-classic-wow))

If you have a trusted alchemist, compare:

> `Stonescale Oil price − vial cost` vs. `Stonescale Eel price`

But since you personally are 300 Cooking, not Alchemy, your cleanest move is raw eel sales.

## 4. Glossy Mightfish

Cook if tanks buy **Cooked Glossy Mightfish**, which gives **+10 Stamina for 10 minutes**. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item%3D13927/cooked-glossy-mightfish))

## 5. Summer Bass

Only cook if there is a premium. Hot Smoked Bass gives **+10 Spirit**, which is often weak demand compared to MP5, Agility, Strength, or Stamina foods. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item%3D13929/hot-smoked-bass))

*   **The Rod:** Big Iron Fishing Pole (+20 Skill) or Nat Pagle's Bone Fishing Pole (+25 Skill).

*   **The Addon:** *Fishing Buddy* (Essential for auto-luring, double-click casting, and tracking catch rates).
*   **The Mindset:** Clear your bags entirely. Leave only your hearthstone, pole, lures, and cooking wood/flint.

### 💰 Chapter 2: The Core Strategies

Here are the three most lucrative, 100% safe, stationary fishing spots in the game, categorized by the time of day you choose to fish.

#### Strategy A: The Degenerate’s Delight (Anytime)
*   **The Catch:** Deviate Fish
*   **The Cook:** Savory Deviate Delight *(Note: You must buy the recipe off the AH, but it pays for itself in an hour).*
*   **The Location:** **Inside the Wailing Caverns Instance.**
    *   *Why here?* The Barrens is Horde territory, but instances are sanctuaries. Run into the Wailing Caverns portal. The moment you are past the instance line, you are 100% safe from PvP. There is a pool of water right at the entrance. You never have to move, and you will never be ganked.
*   **The Market:** Players love turning into Ninjas and Pirates. It is a massive vanity item for PvP and raiding.
*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **25g - 45g** (Highly dependent on server economy, but always in top demand).

#### Strategy B: The Healer's Sustenance (Nighttime: 18:00 - 06:00 Server Time)
*   **The Catch:** Raw Nightfin Snapper
*   **The Cook:** Nightfin Soup (+8 Mana per 5 seconds). Essential for every raiding Priest, Paladin, and Druid.
*   **The Location:** **Jademir Lake, Feralas.**
    *   *Why here?* Most people fish Nightfin in Moonglade or Camp Mojache. Jademir Lake is located in the far northwest corner of Feralas. It is an obscure, hidden lake surrounded by high-level treants. As a level 60 warrior, you can clear one treant, stand on the edge of the water, and literally never be found by Horde players. It has one of the highest drop rates for Nightfin in the game.
    *   *Alternative:* **Moonglade**. It is a sanctuary zone. The guards will obliterate anyone who starts PvP.
*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **30g - 50g** (Cooked Nightfin sells for a massive premium on raid reset days).

#### Strategy C: The Alchemist's Dream (Midnight to Noon: 00:00 - 12:00 Server Time)
*   **The Catch:** Stonescale Eel
*   **The Cook:** Do NOT cook these. Sell them raw. Alchemists need them to make Stonescale Oil, which is required for *Flask of the Titans* (the most expensive tanking flask in the game).
*   **The Location:** **Feathermoon Stronghold, Feralas.**
    *   *Why here?* It is an Alliance town on an island. It is swarming with high-level Alliance guards. Stand on the docks. You have a zero percent chance of being ganked, you don't have to move an inch, and the coastal waters here yield excellent Stonescale Eel catch rates.
*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **35g - 60g** (Stonescale Eels are consistently the most expensive raw fish in WoW Classic).

### ❄️ Chapter 3: The Seasonal Jackpot (Winter Only)
*WoW Classic mimics real-world seasons. From **December 21st to March 19th**, the loot tables change.*

*   **The Catch:** Winter Squid
*   **The Cook:** Grilled Squid (+10 Agility). The absolute Best-in-Slot food for Rogues and Hunters.
*   **The Location:** Feathermoon Stronghold docks (same as the Eels).
*   **The Strategy:** During Winter, fish at the Feathermoon docks between **12:00 PM and 18:00 PM** (when Eels and Nightfin don't spawn). You will catch Winter Squid. Cook them into Grilled Squid.
*   **The Big Brain Play:** Do not sell them in Winter. Bank them on a level 1 alt. Sell them in July and August when they are literally impossible to catch in-game. The price will triple.
*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **40g - 80g+** (If hoarded and sold during the Summer).

### 📊 Chapter 4: The "Lazy Raider Tax" (Auction House Strategy)

Having 300 Cooking is only half the battle; knowing *how* to sell to raiders is the other half. 

1.  **Stack Sizes Matter:** Never sell buff food in stacks of 20. Sell them in **stacks of 5 or 10**. A raider logging on 10 minutes before Molten Core or Naxxramas doesn't want to spend 20g on a stack of 20; they want exactly what they need for the night. You can charge 20-30% more per fish by splitting them into smaller stacks.
2.  **Timing the Market:** Post your auctions on **Tuesday and Wednesday evenings** between 5:00 PM and 7:00 PM server time. This is when 90% of guilds begin their raid week. Prices naturally spike due to desperate, last-minute buyers.
3.  **The Warrior Tax:** While you are fishing, you will catch *Large Raw Mithril Head* and *Raw Redgill*. Cook these into heavily stamina-buffed foods. Mail them to your warrior alt or keep them for your own grinding. It saves you gold on buying your own consumables.

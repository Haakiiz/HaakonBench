- **Human racial**: Perception on if you ever see a stealthed rogue (rare in these spots).

### How to Fish & Cook Simultaneously (Maximum Efficiency)
1. Drop cooking fire.
2. Cast fishing.
3. Every 2–3 stacks of fish, cook on the same fire (no movement).
4. Vendor the cooked food to the nearest guard/innkeeper every 40–60 stacks (they buy at decent prices) or mail to your alt for AH.

**Night rotation (00:00–06:00)**: Southshore river → Nightfin Soup spam. One stack of 20 Nightfin = one stack of Nightfin Soup (widely bought by mages/locks).  
**Day rotation**: Menethil or Theramore → cook oils + filets.

### Gold-per-Hour Reality Check (Fresh Era prices)
- Nightfin Soup route: 90–120 g/hr (peak demand during raid nights).
- Stonescale Eel weeks (when they spike): Menethil can push 100+ g/hr.
- Normal day (oils + cod): 50–70 g/hr average.
- All numbers assume you cook everything. Raw fish sells for ~40 % less.

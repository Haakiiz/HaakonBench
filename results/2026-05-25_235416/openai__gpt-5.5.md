# The “Don’t Move, Don’t Die” Fishing Plan  
**Character:** Level 60 Human Warrior, Alliance  
**Skills:** 300 Fishing / 300 Cooking  
**Goal:** Long stationary fishing sessions, low death risk, strong gold/hour, using Cooking when it actually adds value.

The key principle: **fish open water from safe/guarded docks or hidden low-traffic spots**, not pools. Pool fishing is often better gold/hour, but it requires movement. Your advantage is endurance and safety.

Gold/hour varies heavily by server economy, so I’ll give realistic ranges and explain what to sell raw vs cooked.

---

# 1. Your Best Overall Spot: Feathermoon Stronghold, Feralas

## Location  
**Feathermoon Stronghold, Feralas** — Alliance island town.  
Fish from the docks or shoreline inside/near the Alliance base.

Approximate area: **Feralas, west coast island around 30,43.**

## Why it’s excellent  
- Alliance-controlled town with guards.
- Very low mob danger.
- Low Horde traffic compared to Tanaris/Azshara/STV.
- You can stand still for hours.
- Coastal high-level fish table gives access to good money fish.

## What you’re fishing for  
- **Stonescale Eel** — sell raw, used for Stonescale Oil.
- **Winter Squid** — cook into **Grilled Squid**.
- **Raw Summer Bass** — usually sell raw or cook if your market likes buff food.
- **Raw Spotted Yellowtail** — cook into **Spotted Yellowtail** if profitable.
- Miscellaneous coastal fish.

## Best times  
### Best gold window: Night  
**Server time: 00:00–06:00**  
Target: **Stonescale Eel**

Stonescale Eels are usually strongest at night and are consistently valuable because alchemists need them.

### Seasonal daytime window  
If your server uses original seasonal fish behavior:

- **Winter Squid season:** roughly late September to late March  
  Fish daylight/noon windows for **Winter Squid**, then cook into **Grilled Squid**.

- **Summer Bass season:** roughly late March to late September  
  Summer Bass is usually weaker than Winter Squid but can still sell.

## Cooking usage  
Cook:

- **Raw Winter Squid → Grilled Squid**  
  This is one of your best Cooking conversions. Melee/hunter/rogue/warrior players buy it for the Agility buff.

Usually do **not** cook:

- **Stonescale Eel** — sell raw.
- **Firefin Snapper** if caught — sell raw.
- **Oily Blackmouth** if caught — sell raw.

## Estimated gold/hour  
Assuming normal Classic economy:

| Time | Main Value | Estimated g/hr |
|---|---:|---:|
| 00:00–06:00 | Stonescale Eel | **12–25g/hr** |
| Winter daytime | Grilled Squid + bycatch | **10–22g/hr** |
| Summer daytime | Bass/Yellowtail/bycatch | **6–14g/hr** |
| Random off-hours | Mixed fish | **5–10g/hr** |

**Verdict:** This is probably your best balance of safety, ease, and profit.

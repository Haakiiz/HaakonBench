## Raw vs cooked rule

Before cooking, check:

```text
Cooked food price - AH cut > Raw fish price + cooking opportunity cost
```

If **Raw Nightfin Snapper** sells for 40s each and **Nightfin Soup** sells for 42s each, do not cook. The cooking profit is not worth the relisting risk.

If Raw Nightfin is 25s and Nightfin Soup is 45s, cook everything.

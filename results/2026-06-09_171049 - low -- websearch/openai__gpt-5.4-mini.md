# Best Time to Fish

This part matters a lot.

## Fish seasonally
- **Winter Squid** is only catchable between the **Fall Equinox and Spring Equinox**.  
- **Summer Bass** is only catchable between the **Spring Equinox and Fall Equinox**.  
- They swap seasonally, and both have time-of-day restrictions except in Bay of Storms. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/best-fishing-pools-farming-locations-classic-wow))

# 🎣 The Peaceful Vanguard: A Warrior’s Guide to Wealth

As a Level 60 Warrior, your repair bills are high, and your farming capabilities (compared to Mages or Hunters) are low. However, your 300 Fishing and 300 Cooking are the great equalizers. By combining these, you transform raw materials into high-end raid consumables that every progression guild desperately needs.

## 🎒 Chapter 1: The Warrior’s Tackle Box (Preparation)
Before you plant your boots by the water, you need to prepare. You are going to sit for hours, so bag space and efficiency are paramount.
*   **The Gear:** Big Iron Fishing Pole (or Nat Pagle's Extreme Angler FC-5000), enchanted with +2 Fishing.
*   **The Lures:** Bring a stack of *Aquadynamic Fish Attractors* (+100 Fishing). Even at 300, high-level zones have a "no-get-away" cap of 425. 
*   **The Spices:** Fill at least two bag slots with *Soothing Spices* and *Hot Spices* (bought from cooking vendors). You will cook on location to save bag space.
*   **The Bags:** Equip four 16-slot (or 18-slot) bags. Deposit all your tanking/DPS gear in the bank except your fishing pole and a weapon swap macro in case of emergencies.

## 🗺️ Chapter 2: The Fishing Sanctuaries (Locations & Strategies)

Here are the three ultimate locations where you can plant your character, watch a movie on your second monitor, and never worry about being ganked.

### Strategy A: The Caverns Capitalist (The Wailing Caverns)
**Location:** Inside the Wailing Caverns Instance (The Barrens). 
**Safety Level:** 100% (You are inside an instance; Horde cannot reach you).
**The Catch:** Deviate Fish.
**The Cook:** Savory Deviate Delight.

*   **The Play:** Run into the Barrens, enter the Wailing Caverns instance portal. Right inside the instance, there is a pool of water. You can fish here infinitely. Because you are inside an instance, you are completely immune to Horde gankers.
*   **Why it works:** *Savory Deviate Delight* is the most sought-after cosmetic consumable in the game. Raiders buy stacks of it to turn into pirates or ninjas during boring raids. 
*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **25 - 40g / hour** (Highly dependent on server, but Savory Deviate Delights always sell at a premium). 
*   *Note: You MUST have the Recipe: Savory Deviate Delight. If you don't have it, buy it from the Auction House. It pays for itself in two hours.*

### Strategy B: The Feathermoon Night-Shift (Feralas)
**Location:** Feathermoon Stronghold, Feralas (Alliance Town).
**Safety Level:** 95% (Horde have to swim to an island surrounded by level 60 Elite Alliance Guards to get to you).
**The Catch:** Stonescale Eel & Winter Squid (Seasonal) / Nightfin Snapper.
**The Cook:** Grilled Squid (Squid) / Raw (Eels).

*   **The Play:** Stand on the docks right next to the Feathermoon guards. 
*   **Time of Day Mechanics:**
    *   *Midnight to 12:00 PM:* Catch **Nightfin Snapper**. Cook into *Nightfin Soup* (+8 Mana per 5). Healers and Casters buy these in bulk.
    *   *12:00 PM to Midnight (Winter Only - Sept 23 to March 19):* Catch **Winter Squid**. Cook into *Grilled Squid* (+10 Agility). Melee DPS and Hunters will pay a fortune for these.
    *   *Anytime:* You will also catch **Stonescale Eels**. DO NOT COOK THESE. Sell them raw to Alchemists making *Flask of the Titans*.
*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **35 - 55g / hour** (During peak raid days: Tuesday/Wednesday).

### Strategy C: The Moonglade Retreat
**Location:** Lake Elune'ara, Moonglade.
**Safety Level:** 90% (It is a contested zone, but it functions as a Druid sanctuary. PvP is highly frowned upon, guards are aggressive to attackers, and very few players go here looking for blood).
**The Catch:** Nightfin Snapper & Sunscale Salmon.
**The Cook:** Nightfin Soup & Poached Sunscale Salmon.

*   **The Play:** Find a cozy spot tucked away on the northern edge of the lake, behind some trees. Fish between **12:00 AM and 6:00 AM** server time for the highest drop rate of Nightfin Snapper. 
*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **30 - 45g / hour** (If fishing during the optimal midnight-6AM window).

## 📈 Chapter 3: Market Domination & Economics

To truly impress the server with your wealth, you must understand *when* to sell, not just *what* to sell.

1.  **The Raid Reset Spike:** Never sell your cooked fish on a Friday or Saturday. Hold all your *Nightfin Soup* and *Grilled Squid* in your bank. Post them on the Auction House on **Tuesday afternoon**. Tuesday is raid reset day. Guilds are panicking to buy their consumables, and prices naturally spike by 15-25%.
2.  **The Raw Material Trick:** You will catch a lot of *Oily Blackmouth* and *Firefin Snapper* in lower zones. Do not vendor these. Alchemy alts need them for Free Action Potions and Fire Protection Potions. 
3.  **The "Campfire" Flex:** As a level 60 Warrior, you have the unique ability to clear out a small mob camp, set up a Basic Campfire, and cook your fish right on the spot. This saves massive amounts of bag space.


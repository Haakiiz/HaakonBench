- **Cooked food** for self-healing between catches
- Keep **Intimidating Shout** and **Intercept** ready if something goes bad

- Fish from **docks, small rocks, or hard-to-path spots**

## Best habits:
- Fish during off-hours
- Avoid obvious hubs
- If in contested zones, pick edges and hidden coves
- Don’t fish where raid groups commonly travel

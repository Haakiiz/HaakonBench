## CHAPTER 5: THE "ONE-HANDED" LAZY SETUP (MACROS)

To achieve true stationary bliss, you should be able to fish with one hand while eating, reading, or watching a show.

### Step 1: The "Cast & Interact" Bindings
Go to your **Keybindings** menu in WoW:
1.  Bind **"Target Mouseover"** to **Mouse Wheel Up**.
2.  Bind **"Interact with Target"** to **Mouse Wheel Down**.

### Step 2: The Fishing Macro
Create a macro and place it on your Action Bar (bound to a comfortable key, like `1` or `Spacebar`):

```macro
#showtooltip Fishing
/equip [noequipped:Fishing Poles] Nat Pagle's Extreme Angler FC-5000
/use [noequipped:Fishing Poles] Bright Baubles
/cast Fishing
```
*(This macro will automatically equip your fishing pole, apply your lure if it's missing, and cast your line with a single press).*

### Step 3: The Lazy Execution
1.  Press your Macro key to cast.
2.  Hover your mouse cursor over the bobber.
3.  When you hear the **"Splash"** sound, roll your **Mouse Wheel Down** to instantly reel it in.
4.  Press your Macro key to cast again. 
*You do not even have to look at the screen—just listen for the splash!*

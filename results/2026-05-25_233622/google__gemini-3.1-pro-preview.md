### Chapter 3: The Auction House Art of War

Catching and cooking the fish is only 50% of the battle. To maximize your Gold Per Hour, you must sell like a Goblin.

1.  **The Tuesday/Wednesday Spike:** Raid resets happen on Tuesday. Never sell your Grilled Squid, Nightfin Soup, or Stonescale Eels on a Friday or Saturday. Hold your stock in your bank. Post them on Tuesday afternoon at a **20% markup**. Desperate raiders will buy them without looking at the price.
2.  **Stack Sizing:** 
    *   Sell *Grilled Squid* and *Nightfin Soup* in stacks of **10 or 20**.
    *   Sell *Savory Deviate Delights* in stacks of **5 or 10** (people buy these impulsively, smaller stacks look cheaper).
    *   Sell *Stonescale Eels* in stacks of **10** (Alchemists prefer precise numbers).
3.  **The "Raw vs. Cooked" Audit:** Before you cook your Winter Squid or Nightfin, check the AH. Sometimes, lazy players who want to level their own Cooking profession will pay *more* for the raw fish than the cooked food. If Raw > Cooked, sell them raw and save yourself the time.

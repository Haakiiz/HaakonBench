# The Core Idea

At level 60 with 300 fishing, your best money comes from fishing in spots that combine:

1. **High-value fish**
2. **Minimal competition / safety**
3. **A market that actually buys the fish**
4. **Time-of-day bonuses where applicable**
5. **Cooking into consumables when it adds value**

In Classic, the best “sit and fish forever” money is usually one of these:

- **Nightfin Soup**
- **Winter Squid / Grilled Squid**
- **Stonescale Eels**
- **Sunscale Salmon**
- **Firefin Snapper**
- **Oily Blackmouth**

Not all of these are cooked, but **your Cooking skill lets you convert some fish into raid consumables**, which is where the real value comes from.

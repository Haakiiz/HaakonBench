# Chapter 1: The Safest Serious Gold Spot  
## Feathermoon Stronghold, Feralas

### Location

Fish from the docks or shoreline at **Feathermoon Stronghold**.

This is one of the best places for an Alliance player who wants safety and laziness.

### Why It Is Good

- Alliance town
- Guards nearby
- Very low chance of being killed
- No need to move
- Good high-level coastal fish table
- Convenient flight path
- You can cook at nearby facilities or hearth/cook elsewhere

### What You Catch

Common useful catches include:

- **Raw Glossy Mightfish**
- **Stonescale Eel**
- **Firefin Snapper**
- **Oily Blackmouth**
- **Raw Spotted Yellowtail**
- Stranglekelp and junk occasionally depending on exact water table

### What to Cook

Cook:

- Raw Glossy Mightfish → **Mightfish Steak**
- Raw Spotted Yellowtail → **Spotted Yellowtail**

Usually, Mightfish Steak is the important one.

Do not cook:
- Stonescale Eel
- Firefin Snapper
- Oily Blackmouth

Those are alchemy materials and should usually be sold raw.

### Best Time

For maximum value:

- **Night:** Better for Stonescale Eel
- **Raid evenings:** Better sale prices
- **Tuesday/Wednesday:** Strong AH movement on many servers

### Estimated Gold/Hour

Conservative:
- **8–12g/hr**

Good server:
- **12–18g/hr**

Excellent economy or during high demand:
- **18–22g/hr**

### Safety Rating

**10/10 for Alliance**

This is probably your default “I want to fish for three hours and not think” spot.

# Chapter 2: The Best Cooked Fish Strategy  
## Nightfin Snapper in Moonglade

### Location

Fish in **Moonglade**, around Lake Elune’ara.

Moonglade is neutral/safe and generally very quiet. You can stand at the lake and fish without moving.

### Why It Is Good

- Very safe
- Very quiet
- Nightfin Soup has consistent value
- Stationary fishing is easy
- No hostile mobs nearby if positioned correctly

### What You Catch

Depending on time:

- **Raw Nightfin Snapper**
- **Raw Sunscale Salmon**
- Other inland fish

### Cooking

Cook:

- Raw Nightfin Snapper → **Nightfin Soup**
- Raw Sunscale Salmon → **Poached Sunscale Salmon**

Nightfin Soup is the real prize.

### Best Time

Fish at night.

General rule:

- **6 PM to 6 AM server time:** Nightfin available
- **Midnight to 6 AM:** often best
- **6 AM to 6 PM:** more Sunscale Salmon

If you can only fish one window, fish late night or early morning.

### Estimated Gold/Hour

Daytime:
- **4–8g/hr**

Night:
- **8–16g/hr**

Strong healer-heavy economy:
- **15–20g/hr**

### Safety Rating

**10/10**

This is one of the most relaxing fishing spots in the game.

### Best For

Players who want a safe, cooked-consumable market.

If your server’s healers buy Nightfin Soup aggressively, this can beat Feathermoon.

# Chapter 4: The Hidden Contested-Zone Option  
## Azshara Coastal Fishing

### Summary

Azshara can be very profitable, but it is not as safe as Feathermoon or Moonglade.

It has access to high-level coastal fish, and depending on spot, you can get:

- Stonescale Eel
- Winter Squid / Summer Bass
- Raw Glossy Mightfish
- Firefin Snapper
- Oily Blackmouth
- Raw Spotted Yellowtail

### Why Go There?

Because fewer people fish there, and on some servers Azshara fish tables are excellent.

### The Problem

- Contested zone
- Some dangerous mobs
- Horde may pass through
- Some coastal areas have elite giants or naga nearby
- You may need to find a rock or coastal angle where mobs do not path

### Suggested Style

Look for a tiny piece of coastline where:

- No mobs path near you
- You can cast into open coastal water
- You are not near a quest hub
- You are not near obvious travel roads
- You can put your back to a cliff or rock

Good general areas to scout:

- Western/northwestern Azshara coastline
- Quiet beaches away from naga camps
- Small rock shelves overlooking fishable water
- Areas far from the main road and Timbermaw traffic

### Estimated Gold/Hour

Normal:
- **10–18g/hr**

Good night fishing:
- **15–25g/hr**

With Winter Squid demand:
- **20g+/hr**

### Safety Rating

- PvE server: **7/10**
- PvP server: **4/10 to 6/10**
- If you find a perfect hidden perch: **8/10**

### My Recommendation

Use Azshara only if:
- You are on a PvE server, or
- Your PvP server is low-pop/off-hours, or
- You are willing to scout a truly hidden spot

Otherwise, Feathermoon is better for your stated goal.

# Chapter 12: Auction House Strategy

Fishing gold is made on the Auction House as much as at the water.

## Sell Timing

Post valuable consumables:

- 30–90 minutes before raid times
- Tuesday/Wednesday evenings
- Sunday evenings
- Before world buff windows if your server coordinates them

## Stack Sizes

Use buyer-friendly stacks.

For food:
- Stacks of 5
- Stacks of 10
- Stacks of 20

For alchemy fish:
- Stacks of 10
- Stacks of 20

For high-value fish:
- Mix full stacks and partial stacks

Some players only need 5–10 for a raid. Others buy bulk.

## Check Raw vs Cooked

Always compare:

- Raw Nightfin Snapper vs Nightfin Soup
- Raw Winter Squid vs Grilled Squid
- Raw Glossy Mightfish vs Mightfish Steak

Sometimes lazy cooks create a premium. Sometimes raw fish sells better because other players are leveling cooking.

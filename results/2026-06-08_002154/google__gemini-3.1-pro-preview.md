# The Iron Line: A Level 60 Warrior’s Masterclass in Static Fishing

Greetings, Champion of Stormwind. As a Level 60 Warrior, your repair bills are astronomical, and grinding mobs for raw gold without a pocket healer is a grueling endeavor. You’ve made a brilliant tactical choice: leveraging your 300 Fishing and 300 Cooking to fund your plate armor and consumables. 

Since you want to remain completely stationary, avoid PvP ganking, and maximize your gold-per-hour (GPH), we are going to exploit **Safe Zones**, **Instances**, and **Time-Gated Mechanics**. 

Here is your comprehensive, zero-movement, maximum-profit fishing strategy.

## Chapter I: The Arsenal (Preparation)
Before you plant your boots, you need the right gear. As a level 60, you have access to the best.
*   **The Rod:** Arcanite Fishing Pole (from Stranglethorn Fishing Extravaganza) or Nat Pagle's Bonebreaker. 
*   **The Lure:** Aquadynamic Fish Attractors (+100 Fishing). You don't want a single fish getting away.
*   **The Reagents:** Fill your bags with **Soothing Spices** and **Hot Spices** from cooking vendors. You will be cooking *on location* using a Basic Campfire to save bag space and increase the value of your catch.

## Chapter II: The Sanctuaries (Where & What to Fish)

### Strategy A: The Instanced Goldmine (100% Safe)
**Location:** Wailing Caverns Instance (The Barrens)
**Target:** Deviate Fish 
**Cooked Into:** Savory Deviate Delight (Requires Recipe: Savory Deviate Delight)
**When to Fish:** 24/7, any time of day.

*   **The Setup:** Run into the Barrens, enter the actual Wailing Caverns instance portal. Once inside, clear the first two raptors, walk to the water’s edge, and cast. 
*   **Why it works:** Because you are inside an instance, **it is physically impossible for Horde to gank you.** You can watch a movie on your second monitor and fish for 8 hours without moving your character a single inch.
*   **The Cooking Synergy:** Raw Deviate Fish sell decently, but *Savory Deviate Delights* (which transform the eater into a pirate or ninja) are bought by raiders and PvPers in massive bulks. 
*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **15g - 25g / hour** (Highly dependent on server economy, but incredibly stable).

### Strategy B: The Alliance Fortress (Guarded & Lucrative)
**Location:** Feathermoon Stronghold Docks (Feralas)
**Target:** Nightfin Snapper, Stonescale Eel, Winter Squid (Seasonal)
**Cooked Into:** Nightfin Soup, Grilled Squid
**When to Fish:** 
    *   *Night (00:00 - 06:00 Server Time):* Nightfin Snapper & Stonescale Eels.
    *   *Winter (Sept 23 - March 19):* Winter Squid (Day or Night).

*   **The Setup:** Stand on the docks of Feathermoon Stronghold. You are surrounded by Level 55+ Elite Alliance Guards. If a Horde rogue tries to open on you, the guards will instantly swarm them. You don't even need to switch to Defensive Stance.
*   **The Cooking Synergy:** 
    *   **Nightfin Soup** (+8 Mana per 5 sec) is mandatory for raiding healers. 
    *   **Grilled Squid** (+10 Agility) is the absolute Best-in-Slot raid food for Hunters and Rogues. 
    *   *Note:* Stonescale Eels cannot be cooked, but they are highly prized by Alchemists for Flask of the Titans. Sell these raw.
*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **25g - 40g / hour** (Spikes heavily during raid reset days).

### Strategy C: The Sanctuary of Elune (Peaceful Contested)
**Location:** Moonglade (Anywhere along the lake)
**Target:** Nightfin Snapper, Sunscale Salmon
**Cooked Into:** Nightfin Soup, Poached Sunscale Salmon
**When to Fish:** Nighttime is best for Nightfin.

*   **The Setup:** Moonglade is technically contested, but it functions as a druid sanctuary. PvP is heavily frowned upon, and high-level neutral guards patrol the town. Find a quiet spot on the edge of Lake Elune. 
*   **The Cooking Synergy:** While Sunscale Salmon (+6 HP/sec) is less popular than Nightfin, it still sells to leveling players. The real money here is purely Nightfin Soup.
*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **20g - 30g / hour**.

## Chapter III: The Time Matrix (When to Fish)

To maximize your GPH, you must treat fishing like the stock market. You don't just fish anywhere at any time.

1.  **The Graveyard Shift (Midnight - 6 AM):** Park your Warrior at Feathermoon Stronghold. This is when **Stonescale Eels** and **Nightfin Snapper** spawn rates are at their absolute highest. 
2.  **The Daytime Grind (6 AM - Midnight):** If it is Winter, stay at Feathermoon for **Winter Squid**. If it is Summer, Feathermoon loses its daytime value. Move to **Wailing Caverns** and farm Deviate Fish.
3.  **The Raid Reset Dump:** Do not sell your cooked food on a Friday. Hold your *Grilled Squid* and *Nightfin Soup* in the bank until **Tuesday afternoon** (or whenever your server's raid reset day is). Raiders logging in for Molten Core/Blackwing Lair will buy your food at a 20-30% markup.

# 1) The Core Idea

At level 60 with max fishing/cooking, the best gold usually comes from one of two styles:

### A. Safe, low-risk, semi-AFK fishing
You sit at a dock or guarded shore and fish without worry.

- Lower gold/hour
- Very low death risk
- Great for long sessions while tabbed out, watching something, etc.

### B. High-value night fishing in a contested coastal zone
You fish **Stonescale Eels** and other valuable fish at night from a hidden shoreline.

- Better gold/hour
- More dangerous
- Still can be done with careful positioning

If your priority is **“do not be killed and do not have to move”**, then the best setup is to fish from a **guarded dock or neutral harbor** and use **Cooking** to increase value.

# 6) Cooking vs Raw Selling: What Should You Do?

## Sell raw if:
- the fish is in very high demand already
- the auction house market is active
- you don’t want to deal with deposits/crafting

## Cook before selling if:
- the cooked version is a known raid consumable
- the cooked item sells faster
- your server’s market favors finished goods

### Usually:
- **Nightfin Snapper → cook**
- **Deviate Fish → cook**
- **Stonescale Eel → often sell raw** unless your server has a cooking niche for it
- **Firefin / Oily Blackmouth → often raw is fine**, unless your AH shows strong cooked demand

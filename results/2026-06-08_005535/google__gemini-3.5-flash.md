# THE RETIRED GLADIATOR’S GUIDE TO WEALTH
### A Level 60 Alliance Warrior’s Guide to Lazy, High-Yield Fishing & Cooking in WoW Classic

Welcome, Commander. You’ve braved the depths of Blackrock Mountain, bled in the Alterac Valley, and cleaved your way through countless foes. But plate armor repairs are expensive, and sometimes, a warrior just wants to put down the Arcanite Reaper, light a campfire, cast a line, and watch the gold roll in.

As a Level 60 Human Warrior with 300 Fishing and 300 Cooking, you possess a unique advantage: **high survivability, the Human diplomacy bonus (for cheap recipe buying), and the patience of a raid tank.** 

This guide is designed for **maximum gold with zero movement and zero PvP interference.** We will bypass the highly contested, gank-heavy pools of the Stranglethorn Vale coast and focus on isolated, Alliance-friendly, or mechanically "safe" sanctuaries where you can mute your game, watch a movie on your second monitor, and fish for hours uninterrupted.

### SPOT A: The AFK Fortress – Feathermoon Stronghold Docks (Feralas)
* **Safety Level:** 10/10 (Absolute Safety)
* **When to fish:** 6:00 PM to 6:00 AM (Server Time)
* **What you are catching:** Stonescale Eel, Oily Blackmouth, Firefin Snapper.

```
       [ Feathermoon Fortress ]
             |      |
        [ Docks / Flight Path ] <-- Stand right here next to the Elven Guards!
             |      |
      ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
         ( Ocean Waters )
```

#### Why it works:
Feathermoon Stronghold is an Alliance island fortress. You can stand right next to the flight master or on the wooden docks. If a Horde player somehow manages to flag themselves and attack you, the Level 55+ Elite Sentinel guards will shred them into ground beef before they can pierce your Plate armor. 

#### The Strategy:
At night (6 PM – 6 AM), the spawn rate of **Stonescale Eels** skyrockets to roughly 30% of your total catches in these ocean waters. 
*   **Raw Stonescale Eels** are the core ingredient for *Stonescale Oil*, which Alchemists require for *Flask of the Titans* and *Greater Stoneshield Potions*.
*   **Oily Blackmouth** and **Firefin Snapper** are used for *Free Action Potions* and *Fire Power Elixirs*. 

#### Cooking Profit Loop:
While you cannot cook the Stonescale Eels for profit (they must be sold raw or traded to an Alchemist partner), you can cook any **Raw Glossy Mightfish** you catch here into **Mightfish Steaks** (+10 Stamina food) to sell to tanks.

*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **35 – 45 Gold** (Highly dependent on Stonescale Eel prices on your server).

### SPOT B: The Pacifist’s Paradise – Lake Elune'ara (Moonglade)
* **Safety Level:** 9.5/10 (Guarded by Cenarion Peacekeepers)
* **When to fish:** 6:00 PM to 6:00 AM (Server Time)
* **What you are catching:** Nightfin Snapper, Raw Redgill.

```
                  [ Nighthaven ]
                        |
             ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
            ~   Lake Elune'ara       ~  <-- Sit on the southern shore
            ~                        ~      under the trees.
             ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
```

#### Why it works:
Moonglade is a neutral druid sanctuary. The **Cenarion Peacekeepers** here are Level 65 Elites who cast a massive Moonfire that deals thousands of damage and silences anyone who initiates PvP. PvP is essentially non-existent here. You can sit on the grassy shores of the lake, entirely undisturbed.

#### The Strategy:
Between 6:00 PM and 6:00 AM, Lake Elune'ara becomes a goldmine for **Nightfin Snapper** (up to a 25-30% catch rate). 

#### Cooking Profit Loop:
This is where your 300 Cooking shines. 
1.  Catch **Raw Nightfin Snapper**.
2.  Use your Warrior's *Cooking* skill to turn them into **Nightfin Soup** (requires 1x Nightfin Snapper, 1x Refreshing Spring Water).
3.  **The Value:** Nightfin Soup restores HP and grants **+8 Mana per 5 seconds (MP5)**. This is the absolute Best-in-Slot raid food for Holy Priests, Resto Druids, Resto Shamans, and Holy Paladins. 
4.  Raiders buy these in stacks of 20 every single week. By cooking them, you double the value of the raw fish.

*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **40 – 55 Gold** (Selling cooked Nightfin Soup in stacks of 20 on Raid Reset days—Tuesdays/Wednesdays).

### SPOT C: The Secret Hermit – The Shattered Strand (Azshara)
* **Safety Level:** 7.5/10 (Contested zone, but functionally empty)
* **When to fish:** 12:00 PM to 6:00 PM (Server Time) – *Must be Autumn/Winter (Sept 23 – March 19)*
* **What you are catching:** Winter Squid, Large Raw Mightfish, Stonescale Eel.

```
     [ Talrendis Point ] (Alliance Camp)
             |
     [ Scalebelly Cliffs ] 
             |  (Slide down the southern cliffs to the hidden beach)
             v
      ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
     ~   The Shattered Strand   ~ <-- You are completely hidden here.
      ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
```

#### Why it works:
Azshara is notoriously annoying to navigate, meaning very few players run around here. If you slide down the cliffs south of Talrendis Point (the Alliance flight path) to the sandy beaches of the Shattered Strand, you will be completely isolated. No one grinds mobs here, and Horde players rarely venture to this specific coastline.

#### The Strategy:
This is a seasonal, time-sensitive goldmine. **Winter Squid** can only be caught during the real-world winter months (September 23 to March 19) and peaks between 12:00 PM and 6:00 PM server time. 

#### Cooking Profit Loop:
1.  Catch **Winter Squid**.
2.  Cook them into **Grilled Squid** (requires 1x Winter Squid, 1x Soothing Spices).
3.  **The Value:** Grilled Squid grants **+10 Agility** for 10 minutes. This is the absolute Best-in-Slot food buff for Rogues, Hunters, and Feral Druids. 
4.  *Pro-Tip:* If you hold onto your cooked Grilled Squid and sell them during the Summer months (when they cannot be caught), the price triples on the Auction House.

*   **Estimated Gold/Hour:** **50 – 70 Gold** (Higher if saved and sold during the Summer phase).

## SUMMARY OF YOUR WEEKLY ROUTINE

To maximize your gold per hour without ever leaving your fishing chair, structure your week like this:

1.  **The Midday Shift (12 PM - 6 PM):** Park your Warrior at **The Shattered Strand (Azshara)**. Catch Winter Squid. Cook them into **Grilled Squid**.
2.  **The Night Shift (6 PM - 12 AM):** Fly to **Moonglade**. Sit by the lake, catch Nightfin Snappers, and cook them into **Nightfin Soup**.
3.  **The Late Night AFK (12 AM - 2 AM):** Hearth to **Feathermoon Stronghold**. Stand next to the guards on the dock. Catch **Stonescale Eels** while half-asleep.
4.  **Tuesdays (Raid Reset):** Post your cooked soups and squids on the Auction House in stacks of 20. Price them 10% higher than the raw fish. Watch your mailbox fill with gold.

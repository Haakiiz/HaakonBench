## CHAPTER 4: The Culinary Goldmine (Auction House Strategy)

To make the absolute most gold per hour, you cannot simply dump your fish on the Auction House at random times. You must think like a cartel leader.

### 1. The Raid Night Premium
*   **Do not post your food on Friday, Saturday, or Monday.** These are low-demand days. Prices will be undercut.
*   **Post your food on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday afternoons (3 PM to 6 PM server time).** This is when raiders log on, realize they forgot their buffs, and panic-buy consumables at inflated prices.

### 2. Packaging Matters
*   Always post your cooked food in **Stacks of 5** or **Stacks of 10**. 
*   Raiders rarely want to buy a single fish, and they rarely want to buy a stack of 20 if they only need a few for a single raid night. Stacks of 5 and 10 sell at a premium.

### 3. The "Double-Dip" Alchemist Partnership
*   If you fish up **Stonescale Eels**, do not just post them raw if you can avoid it. Find a trusted Alchemist in your guild. 
*   Have them craft **Stonescale Oil** or **Greater Stoneshield Potions**. Sell the finished potions on raid nights for a 20-30% markup over the raw eel price.

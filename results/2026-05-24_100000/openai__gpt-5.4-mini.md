1. **Fishing pools**
2. **Fishing at the correct time/weather/location**
3. **Cooking only the fish with real market demand**
4. **Selecting places with zero danger and low competition**

On Classic servers, the correct move is often:
- **Check your Auction House**
- Compare the raw fish price vs cooked item price
- If cooked adds little value, sell raw
- If cooked creates a raid consumable or novelty item, cook it

1.  **Raid Day Price Gouging:** Do not sell your *Nightfin Soup* or *Grilled Squid* on a Monday. Sell them on **Tuesday and Wednesday afternoons** (right before peak raiding hours). Prices naturally spike by 15-20% as desperate guild masters buy out the AH.
2.  **Stack Sizing:**

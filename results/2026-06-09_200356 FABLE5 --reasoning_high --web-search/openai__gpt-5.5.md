# 1. The clock matters more than people think

WoW Classic fishing has time bands. The important ones:

| Server time | Best target |
|---:|---|
| **12:00 a.m.–6:00 a.m.** | **Nightfin Snapper**, **Stonescale Eel** |
| **6:00 a.m.–12:00 p.m.** | Some Nightfin/Stonescale still possible, plus Sunscale |
| **12:00 p.m.–6:00 p.m.** | Sunscale; seasonal coastal fish |
| **6:00 p.m.–12:00 a.m.** | Nightfin/Stonescale ramp back in |

Wowhead’s Classic fishing guide notes the four fishing time windows and that Nightfin Snapper and Stonescale Eel are dramatically better at night; server time is what matters. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/fishing-leveling-1-300-wow-classic))

### Current-date note: June 9, 2026
Right now, you are in the **Raw Summer Bass** half of the coastal seasonal cycle, not Winter Squid season. Winter Squid returns around the fall equinox window, roughly **September 22/23, 2026**, and runs until around **March 19/20, 2027**. Raw Summer Bass is the opposite-cycle fish. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/best-fishing-pools-farming-locations-classic-wow))

So today, your best gold is usually **Stonescale Eel at night**, **Nightfin Soup at night**, or **Savory Deviate Delight anytime**. Winter Squid becomes a major play again in late September.

**The “I don’t want to see another player” pick.**

### Where to stand
Enter **Wailing Caverns**, clear to a safe water edge, and fish open water. As a level 60 warrior, the mobs are trivial. Once your pocket is clear, you can stand still and fish.

### What you’re farming
- **Deviate Fish**
- Cook into **Savory Deviate Delight**

Deviate Fish can be caught in Wailing Caverns waters, including inside the dungeon, and open-water Wailing Caverns fishing is listed around a **30% Deviate Fish** catch rate. ### When
Any time. No night/day/season dependency.

### Cooking plan
You need **Recipe: Savory Deviate Delight**. If you do not already have it, buy it from the AH. Cook every Deviate Fish if:

> **Savory Deviate Delight price > Deviate Fish price + Mild Spices + AH cut**

Savory Deviate Delight requires only low cooking skill, and the recipe is associated with Northern Barrens drops; El’s lists the food’s pirate/ninja transformation value and the Deviate Fish ingredient. ([elsanglin.com](https://www.elsanglin.com/fish/savory_deviate_delight.html))

### Estimated g/hr
Assume 130–160 catches/hr and ~30% Deviate:
- ~39–48 Deviate Fish/hr.
- If Savory sells for 20s: **8–10g/hr**
- If Savory sells for 50s: **20–25g/hr**
- If Savory sells for 1g+: **40–50g+/hr**

### Why this is secretly excellent
It is not the theoretical highest g/hr, but it is one of the best **safety-adjusted** farms in the game:
- No roaming pool route.
- No enemy players inside.
- No high-level mobs once cleared.

If you want to fish while watching a movie and never move your character, this is elite.

# 4. What to cook, what to sell raw

## Always check raw vs cooked before mass-cooking

Use this simple rule:

> **Cook only if cooked unit price > raw unit price + vendor reagent + AH cut.**

### Stonescale Eel
**Sell raw** unless you have an alchemist converting to Stonescale Oil at a better margin. Stonescale Eel is a Classic alchemy reagent, and its value comes from alchemist/raid demand, not cooking. Wowhead and Warcraft Wiki-style references consistently describe it as a high-value reagent used for Stonescale Oil and high-level consumables. ([warcraft.wiki.gg](https://warcraft.wiki.gg/wiki/Stonescale_Eel?utm_source=openai))

### Raw Nightfin Snapper
Usually **cook into Nightfin Soup** if your server’s healers buy cooked food. Nightfin Soup gives +8 mana per 5 seconds for 10 minutes and uses Raw Nightfin Snapper plus Refreshing Spring Water. ([dotesports.com](https://dotesports.com/wow/news/how-to-craft-nightfin-soup-in-wow-classic-wow-sod))

### Winter Squid
Usually **cook into Grilled Squid**, especially during or after Winter Squid season. Grilled Squid gives +10 Agility for 10 minutes and requires Winter Squid plus Soothing Spices. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item%3D13942/recipe-grilled-squid))

### Raw Summer Bass
Cook into **Hot Smoked Bass** only if spirit food sells on your realm. It is much less universally desirable than Grilled Squid.

### Deviate Fish
Cook into **Savory Deviate Delight** if you have the recipe and the cooked price is higher. This is often a strong Cooking value-add because people buy the finished novelty food impulsively.

### Firefin Snapper / Oily Blackmouth
Usually **sell raw** to alchemists. If you have an alchemist friend, compare raw fish vs Fire Oil / Blackmouth Oil.

# 8. The 30-minute realm calibration test

Before committing to a spot forever, do this:

1. Pick the spot and correct time window.
2. Fish for exactly **30 minutes**.
3. Count each valuable fish.
4. Multiply by 2.
5. Use your AH unit prices.

Formula:

```text
Gold/hour =
(Stonescale/hour × eel price)
+ (Nightfin/hour × cooked-or-raw price)
+ (Squid/hour × cooked-or-raw price)
+ (Deviate/hour × savory-or-raw price)
+ bycatch value
- lure/spice/water costs
```

If your realm has:
- **Stonescale Eel > 1.5g each:** Feathermoon/Steamwheedle/Azshara wins.
- **Nightfin Soup > 75s each:** Moonglade wins at night.
- **Savory Deviate Delight > 75s each:** Wailing Caverns becomes absurdly good for its safety.
- **Winter Squid / Grilled Squid > 1g each:** during season, fish coastal midday and bank stock.

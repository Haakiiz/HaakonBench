### Chapter 1: Prep & Loadout (5–10 Minutes)
- **Gear**: Any fishing pole (Strong Fishing Pole or better; buy from vendors). Stock **Bright Baubles** (+75 fishing, cheap from fishing vendors) or Aquadynamic Fish Attractors. Wear +fishing gear if you have it (e.g., from quests). Human racial helps slightly with spirit, but irrelevant here.
- **Find Fish** (if available via Weather-Beaten Journal from wreckage pools): Shows pools on minimap—huge QoL for efficiency.
- **Inventory**: Empty bags or dedicated fishing/cooking bags. Bring Refreshing Spring Water (for soups), spices if needed for recipes, and a hearthstone/mount for quick returns.
- **Cooking Synergy**: Level 300 lets you make top raid foods like Nightfin Soup (+8 MP5, BiS for casters), Sagefish Delight (mana regen), Grilled Squid (+10 Agility), etc. Always cook high-demand items rather than vendoring raw fish when profitable.
- **Warrior AFK Safety**: Sit in a defensive spot (use /sit macro or just park). As a Warrior, you’re tanky— but avoid aggro. Bind “Fishing” and have a “Stop Casting” macro. Watch for rare ganks; log off or hearth if needed. No movement required once positioned.

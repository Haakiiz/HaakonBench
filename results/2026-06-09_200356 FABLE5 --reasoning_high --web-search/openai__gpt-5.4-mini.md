## The big idea

If you want the **best mix of safety + stationary fishing + profit**, your two best homes are:

1. **Steamwheedle Port, Tanaris** — your practical main base. It’s neutral, mostly surrounded by non-hostile turtles, and Gikkix there sells the key fish recipes you care about. The nearby coast gives you **Stonescale Eel**, **Raw Spotted Yellowtail**, **Raw Glossy Mightfish**, and seasonally **Winter Squid** / **Summer Bass**. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/best-fishing-pools-farming-locations-classic-wow))

2. **Moonglade** — your secret “night-fishing sanctuary” **if you can access it**. Nightfin can be fished there, and the zone is described as having very few hostile mobs; there’s also an all-class Alliance flight path mentioned in the comments. If you can get in, this is the closest Classic gets to a “fish forever and never get bothered” spot. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/best-fishing-pools-farming-locations-classic-wow))

### B. **Moonglade — best “no-kill, no-drama” Nightfin bunker**
If you can access it, Moonglade is the dream. The zone is described by players as having little if any hostile mobs, and Nightfin can be fished there. There’s also an Alliance flight path mentioned at **48,67**. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/zone%3D493/moonglade))

**Use this if:** you want the lowest risk possible and your server route to Moonglade isn’t annoying.

### C. **Feathermoon Outpost, Feralas — Alliance backup**
Classic comments on the Stonescale page specifically call out **Feathermoon Outpost** as the Alliance Stonescale spot. If Tanaris is crowded, this is your fallback. ([wowhead.com](https://www.wowhead.com/classic/item%3D13422/stonescale-eel?utm_source=openai))

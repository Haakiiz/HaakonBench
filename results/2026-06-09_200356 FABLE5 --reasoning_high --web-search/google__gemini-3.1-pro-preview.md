In WoW Classic, fishing is dictated by a strict internal clock. To maximize gold, you cannot fish blindly; you must fish during specific "shifts." Furthermore, your 300 Cooking is your profit multiplier. Raw fish sell decently, but raid-buff food sells at a premium to min-maxing raiders.

*   **The Day Shift (12:00 PM - 6:00 PM server time):** Used primarily for catching *Winter Squid* (if fishing during the winter months) or *Sunscale Salmon*.

*   Hoard a large supply in your bank. When Summer hits, Winter Squid becomes completely unobtainable. By July, the price of Grilled Squid will double or triple on the Auction House because Rogues and Hunters still need their +10 Agility raid buffs.

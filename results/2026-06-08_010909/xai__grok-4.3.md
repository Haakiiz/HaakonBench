**The Ironclad Angler’s Compendium**  
*Level 60 Human Warrior – Classic Fishing Doctrine*  
*“Stand. Cast. Profit. Repeat.”*

You want zero movement, zero corpse runs, and maximum gold per hour while leveraging 300/300 Fishing + Cooking. This guide is built exclusively around Alliance-controlled or extremely low-traffic pockets where a warrior can fish for 4–8 hours straight without ever needing to move more than 10 yards.

### 1. Primary Sanctuaries (Zero Risk, High Consistency)

**A. Theramore Docks – Dustwallow Marsh (Recommended #1)**  
- **Safety**: Theramore is Alliance territory. Horde rarely shows up unless they bring a full raid. You can stand on the southern dock (the one facing the open water) and never get attacked.  
- **Position**: Stand at the very end of the southernmost dock, facing southeast. You will fish open water + occasional pools that drift in.  
- **Target Fish**:  
  - Nightfin Snapper (night only)  
  - Stonescale Eel (day & night)  
  - Raw Rockscale Cod / Firefin Snapper (day)  
- **Best Window**: 8 PM – 6 AM server time (Nightfin + Eel overlap).  
- **Estimated Gold/Hour**: **28–42g**  
  - Raw Nightfin: 1.8–3.2g each  
  - Stonescale Eel: 2.5–4g each  
  - Cooked Nightfin Soup stacks sell for 9–14g depending on server economy.  
- **Cooking Play**: Turn every Nightfin into Nightfin Soup. The soup sells faster and for more than raw fish on most servers.

**B. Feathermoon Stronghold – Feralas (Highest Consistent Value)**  
- **Safety**: Full Alliance outpost with guards. You can fish for literally hours without seeing another player.  
- **Position**: Stand on the small wooden platform just south of the flight master, facing the bay. This spot has excellent pool density.  
- **Target Fish**:  
  - Greater Sagefish (pools)  
  - Nightfin Snapper (night)  
  - Stonescale Eel  
- **Best Window**: Day for Sagefish pools, night for Nightfin.  
- **Estimated Gold/Hour**: **35–52g**  
  - Raw Greater Sagefish: 2.8–5g each  
  - Sagefish Delight sells for 6–11g per stack.  
- **Why it wins**: Sagefish Delight is consumed by every raid healer and caster. Steady demand.

**C. Quel’Danil Lodge – The Hinterlands**  
- **Safety**: Tiny Alliance outpost with guards. Extremely low traffic.  
- **Position**: Fish from the shore directly in front of the lodge, facing the water.  
- **Target Fish**: Greater Sagefish pools + Nightfin at night.  
- **Estimated Gold/Hour**: **30–45g**  
  - Slightly lower volume than Feathermoon but very peaceful.

### 2. Temporal Optimization (Day/Night Cycle)

- **Night (most profitable)**: Prioritize Nightfin Snapper everywhere. Nightfin Soup is the single best gold-per-fish item you can produce.  
- **Day**: Focus on Greater Sagefish pools (Feathermoon/Hinterlands) and Stonescale Eel.  
- **Pro move**: Log in at 7:45 PM, fish until 2 AM, then switch to daytime Sagefish. This single schedule usually nets the highest 8-hour session.

### 3. Cooking Synergy – The Real Money Printer

### 4. Hidden / Semi-Contested Spots (Medium Risk, Higher Reward)

**The “Quiet Rock” – Bay of Storms, Azshara**  
Stand on the large flat rock formation on the western edge of the bay (coordinates roughly 48, 50 area). You can fish the high-density Sagefish and Eel pools while being almost invisible from the main road.  
- Risk: Low-to-medium (occasional Horde passing through).  
- Estimated Gold/Hour: **48–68g** when pools are up.  
- Only use this if you’re willing to accept a 5–10% chance of needing to fight or log.

### 5. Warrior-Specific Protocols

- **Never move**: Use a fishing macro that casts and auto-loots. You only need to click once every 20–30 seconds.  
- **Mount up, not away**: Keep your mount summoned so you can instantly mount and ride 10 yards if a rare ganker appears (extremely unlikely at these spots).  
- **Bait**: Use Aquadynamic Fish Attractor when pools are visible. Otherwise, just fish.  
- **Inventory management**: Keep one bag slot open. Vendor trash fish (Bass, Cod) every 45–60 minutes at the local Alliance vendor so you never have to move far.  
- **Session length**: These locations are designed for 4–8 hour marathons. The boredom factor is real — have music or a second monitor ready.

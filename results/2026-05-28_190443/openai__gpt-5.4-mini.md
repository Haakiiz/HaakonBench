# The Core Idea

As a level 60 human warrior, your fishing income comes from **three layers of value**:

1. **Raw fish sales**  
   Fast, easy, reliable.
2. **Cooked fish value**  
   Some fish become significantly more valuable once cooked into stat food.
3. **Secondary loot**  
   Turtles, chests, herbs, stonescale eel, oily blackmouth, bottles, etc. can add meaningful bonus gold.

Your biggest edge is that you can fish in places where:
- mobs are irrelevant or never aggro
- other players don’t bother checking
- fish types are highly marketable
- you can remain seated for long sessions without moving

Feralas is one of the best zones for a level 60 Alliance fisher because it has:

- Mobs are avoidable and typically not a direct threat

- Sparse traffic
- Good for long sessions
- Can often remain in one small fishing position for extended periods

- **Sunscale Salmon**
- occasional other valuable inland catches

- Nightfin Snapper -> **Nightfin Soup**
- Sunscale Salmon -> often sold raw or cooked depending on server demand

### Gold/hour estimate

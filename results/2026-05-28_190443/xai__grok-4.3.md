### Tier List of Eternal Spots

**S-Tier: Southshore River (Hillsbrad Foothills) – Recommended Main Base**
- Exact spot: Just south of Southshore, on the eastern bank of the river (coords ~58, 70). You sit literally inside Alliance guard patrol range.
- Fish: Raw Nightfin Snapper (night) + Raw Sunscale Salmon (day) + Raw Greater Sagefish
- Schedule: 21:00–06:00 server time for Nightfin (best gold). 06:00–21:00 for Sunscale/Sagefish.
- Cooking loop: Turn Nightfin into Nightfin Soup (resto pots), Sagefish into Sagefish Delight (cast speed food). Both sell extremely well.
- Gold estimate: 28–38 g/hr (Nightfin Soup dominates). Lower pop servers can hit 45 g/hr.

**S-Tier: Menethil Harbor Hidden Jetty (Wetlands)**
- Exact spot: The small wooden dock directly west of the inn, tucked against the mountain (coords ~10, 57). Guards walk within 30 yd.
- Fish: Oily Blackmouth + Firefin Snapper + Stonescale Eel (rare pools)
- Schedule: Any time. Blackmouth/Firefin are consistent day or night.
- Cooking loop: Blackmouth Oil + Fire Oil (huge herb/alch demand).
- Gold estimate: 22–32 g/hr steady. Spikes to 40 g/hr when Stonescale procs.

**A-Tier: The Loch (Loch Modan) – Absolute Zero Risk**
- Spot: Northwest shore near Thelsamar (coords ~48, 18). You are inside a major Alliance town.
- Fish: Raw Bristle Whisker Catfish + Raw Mithril Head Trout
- Use: Turn into Mithril Head Trout for mid-tier food or sell raw. Not the highest value, but you can literally AFK here for 12 hours.
- Gold estimate: 12–18 g/hr (best “brain-off” option).

**A-Tier: Darkshire Duskwood River (Hidden Bend)**
- Spot: The river bend directly east of Darkshire, behind the tree line (coords ~75, 47). Town guards still have line of sight.
- Fish: Raw Nightfin Snapper + Deviate Fish (very rare but sells for surprising money).
- Gold estimate: 18–25 g/hr.

### Chapter 2: The Graveyard Shift (Nightfin Snapper)
*Healers are addicted to Nightfin Soup (+8 Mana per 5 sec). They will pay whatever you ask.*

*   **The Location:** **Moonglade** (The lake in the center) or **Camp Feathermoon, Feralas**.

*   **The Time:** 00:00 (Midnight) to 06:00 AM server time. This is when the Nightfin drop rate spikes to nearly 40% of your catches.
*   **The Strategy:** Stand on the dock at Feathermoon or the quiet edge of Moonglade. Cast repeatedly. You will catch Raw Nightfin Snapper and Raw Sunscale Salmon.
*   **The Cooking Synergy:** 
    *   Cook the Nightfin into **Nightfin Soup** (Recipe sold by Vivianna in Feathermoon Stronghold).
    *   Cook the Sunscale into **Poached Sunscale Salmon** (Recipe sold by Vivianna).
*   **Estimated Gold Per Hour:** **25g - 40g / hour**. (Nightfin Soup sells for premium prices on raid days, usually Tuesday/Wednesday).

### Chapter 3: The Instanced Goldmine (Savory Deviate Delight)
*Everyone wants to look like a Ninja or a Pirate. This is the most consistent, year-round gold farm in the game.*

*   **The Location:** **Inside the Wailing Caverns Instance (The Barrens).** 
    *   *Why:* The Barrens is Horde territory, but if you walk *into* the actual Wailing Caverns dungeon portal, you are in an instance. You are 100% safe from Horde. You do not need to pull any mobs; there is a pool of water right at the entrance.
*   **The Time:** 24/7. Time of day does not affect Deviate Fish.
*   **The Strategy:** Stand at the instance entrance. Cast into the instanced water. Your catch rate for Deviate Fish will be roughly 30%.
*   **The Cooking Synergy:** You *must* have the **Recipe: Savory Deviate Delight**. It is a rare world drop in the Barrens. Buy it off the Neutral AH or Alliance AH if you don't have it. It is an investment that pays for itself in two hours.
*   **Estimated Gold Per Hour:** **20g - 35g / hour**. (Cooked Savory Deviates sell incredibly fast to PvP twinks and raiders).

### Chapter 4: The Hidden Cliff (Winter Squid)
*Melee DPS (including you) and Hunters crave Grilled Squid (+10 Agility). It is the absolute best food buff for physical damage.*

*   **The Location:** **Azshara - The Shattered Strand (Hidden Ledge).**
    *   *Why:* Azshara is contested, but it is massive and empty. Head to the cliffs above the Bay of Storms (around coordinates 54, 48). Do not go to the beach. Find a high cliff edge where you can *just* reach the water with your bobber. Horde players farming essence/herbs below will never look up to see you. You are practically invisible.
*   **The Time:** **Seasonal.** Winter Squid can *only* be caught between September 23rd and March 19th. They bite best between 12:00 PM (Noon) and 06:00 PM.
*   **The Strategy:** Cast into the open water. No need to look for pools. You will catch Winter Squid and Raw Glossy Mightfish.
*   **The Cooking Synergy:** 
    *   Cook into **Grilled Squid** (Recipe sold by Gikkix in Steamwheedle Port, Tanaris). 
    *   *Pro-Tip:* If you catch these near the end of the season (March), hold onto them in your bank. By July, the price of Grilled Squid triples because the supply dries up.
*   **Estimated Gold Per Hour:** **35g - 60g / hour** (During peak raid hours or off-season selling).

# The Ironclad Angler

### A WoW Classic fishing & cooking gold guide for the level 60 Human Warrior

*300 Fishing · 300 Cooking · zero deaths · zero walking*

> 🖥️ **Prefer it visual?** Open [`guide.html`](guide.html) in a browser — same
> guide with a live window-tracker clock, 24-hour timeline, and gold/hour
> charts. No build step, just open the file.

You are a warrior. You have no Vanish, no Blink, no Feign Death — you have a
hearthstone, plate armor, and a bad attitude. This guide is built around that
reality: every primary spot is either inside an Alliance garrison's guard
radius or so remote that nobody will ever find you. You park, you cast, you
get rich.

All fish, recipes, vendors, and time windows below are patch 1.12 verified.

## Chapter 1 — The Golden Rule

**Fish what raiders eat and alchemists drink.** Everything else is vendor
trash with extra steps. At 300/300 there are exactly four money fish that
matter, plus two alchemy fish:

| Fish | Why it sells | Your 300 Cooking play |
|------|-------------|----------------------|
| **Raw Nightfin Snapper** | → **Nightfin Soup** (+8 mana per 5 sec, 10 min) — every healer and caster in every raid wants this | Cook it (250 skill). The soup sells for more than the fish |
| **Stonescale Eel** | Alchemy: **Flask of the Titans** and Greater Stoneshield Potion. Tank flasks never go out of style | Sell raw — eels are an alchemy reagent, not food |
| **Raw Winter Squid** *(seasonal)* | → **Grilled Squid** (+10 Agility, 10 min) — rogues, hunters, and fellow warriors | Cook it (240 skill) |
| **Large Raw Mightfish** | → **Mightfish Steak** (+10 Stamina/+10 Spirit) | Cook it (175 skill) |
| **Oily Blackmouth** | Alchemy: Blackmouth Oil → Free Action Potions (PvP demand) | Sell raw, stacks of 20 |
| **Firefin Snapper** | Alchemy: Fire Oil | Sell raw |

Honorable mention: **Raw Whitescale Salmon** → **Baked Salmon** (+12 Stam/+12
Spirit, 275 skill) and **Zesty Clam Meat** → **Undermine Clam Chowder** (225
skill) round out your catch instead of being junk.

## Chapter 2 — Gear up before you cast

One shopping trip, then never again:

1. **Pole:** Big Iron Fishing Pole (+20) is fine. The **Arcanite Fishing
   Pole** (+35) is the dream — see Chapter 6 on the Stranglethorn
   Extravaganza.
2. **Lures:** **Aquadynamic Fish Attractor** (+100 for 10 min) for high-end
   water; **Bright Baubles** (+75) as the cheap workhorse. At 300 skill +
   pole + lure you sit at ~400 effective — zero getaways everywhere in
   Azeroth.
3. **Recipes — buy these once:**
   - **Gikkix**, Steamwheedle Port, Tanaris (neutral goblin town, safe to
     visit): *Nightfin Soup*, *Grilled Squid*, *Poached Sunscale Salmon*,
     *Spotted Yellowtail*.
   - **Vivianna**, Feathermoon Stronghold, Feralas: *Mightfish Steak*,
     *Baked Salmon*.
   - Tanaris/Booty Bay vendors: *Undermine Clam Chowder* (Jabbey in Tanaris
     or Mrs. Gant in Booty Bay).
4. **Macro** (one button, no fumbling):
   ```
   /equip Big Iron Fishing Pole
   /cast Fishing
   ```
   Keep your weapon hotkeyed for the swap back — you're still a warrior.
5. Confirm you've done **Nat Pagle's Artisan Fishing quest** (Dustwallow
   Marsh — sail from Theramore, an Alliance town). Without it you're capped
   at 225 and none of this works.

## Chapter 3 — The clock and the calendar

Fishing gold is time-gated. Internalize these three windows:

### 🌙 The Nightfin window — 6 PM to 6 AM server time
Raw Nightfin Snapper **only bites at night**. This is the single most
important fact in this guide. Daytime in the same water gives you Raw
Sunscale Salmon instead (fine, sells worse).

### 🌗 The Stonescale window — 12 AM to 12 PM
Stonescale Eel catch rates are markedly higher from midnight to noon.

### 📅 The seasonal axis
- **Raw Winter Squid:** September 23 → March 20 only.
- **Raw Summer Bass:** roughly March 20 → September 22.

**The two windows overlap from midnight to 6 AM.** That's the power hour
block: Nightfin *and* boosted Stonescale at the same time. If you only fish a
few hours a week, fish then.

## Chapter 4 — The spot dossiers

Each spot is rated for **Safety** (will anything kill an AFK-ish warrior?)
and **Boredom-proofing** (mailbox? vendor? forge?).

---

### 🏰 Dossier 1: Feathermoon Stronghold dock — Feralas
**Safety: ★★★★★ (Alliance town, guards on the pier) · Convenience: ★★★★★**

The crown jewel. You stand on the dock of an Alliance garrison — flight
master, innkeeper, vendors, mailbox, and the recipe vendor Vivianna a short
walk away. Nothing can touch you. Cast off the pier into the coastal water.

- **Catch:** Stonescale Eel (best 12 AM–12 PM), **Large Raw Mightfish**
  (this coast is one of its homes), Raw Spotted Yellowtail, seasonal squid
  or bass, clams.
- **Cooking play:** Mightfish → Mightfish Steak on the spot (Vivianna sells
  the recipe 50 yards away). Mail eels to your bank alt from the stronghold
  mailbox without moving more than ten steps.
- **Estimated:** **8–14 g/h raw, 10–16 g/h with cooking.** During the
  midnight–noon eel window, the high end.

---

### 🌲 Dossier 2: Jademir Lake — northern Feralas
**Safety: ★★★★☆ (contested, but nobody on your server knows it exists) · Convenience: ★☆☆☆☆**

A hidden mountain lake in Feralas' far north — no road leads there, no quest
sends anyone there. In hundreds of hours you might see one druid. This is the
premier **Nightfin Snapper** water on Kalimdor, with Raw Redgill and Raw
Mithril Head Trout as filler.

- **When:** 6 PM – 6 AM. Outside the window, go to Dossier 1 instead.
- **Catch:** Raw Nightfin Snapper (the money), Raw Sunscale Salmon (day),
  Redgill, Mithril Head Trout.
- **Cooking play:** Carry Refreshing Spring Water (innkeeper item — stock
  up at Feathermoon). Cook **Nightfin Soup** at your campfire between bites.
  Soup sells in 5-stacks to raiders for noticeably more than raw fish.
- **Estimated:** **9–15 g/h raw, 12–18 g/h cooked.** Top earner per cast in
  this guide.
- **Warrior note:** clear of mobs at the waterline; bring food, there's no
  vendor for a zone and a half. **Sardor Isle** (southwest Feralas coast)
  is the saltwater alternative with the same night window.

---

### ❄️ Dossier 3: Lake Kel'Theril — Winterspring
**Safety: ★★★★☆ (contested, but Winterspring is a ghost town; lakeshore mobs are sparse) · Convenience: ★★★☆☆**

Everwinter ambience, Nightfin at night, and Everlook (neutral goblin town,
mailbox + vendor + AH-adjacent services) ten seconds up the hill. Horde
almost never patrol here, and you're 60 — the wildlife is grey to you anyway.

- **When:** 6 PM – 6 AM for Nightfin.
- **Catch:** Raw Nightfin Snapper, Raw Redgill.
- **Estimated:** **8–13 g/h raw, 11–16 g/h as soup.**

---

### 🌳 Dossier 4: Lake Elune'ara — Moonglade
**Safety: ★★★★★ (the most peaceful zone in the game — hostile players are functionally absent, hostile mobs entirely so) · Convenience: ★★★☆☆**

If "I must never die, ever" is the binding constraint, this is your answer.
Moonglade has no hostile wildlife near the lake and effectively no Horde
traffic. As a warrior you'll need the long walk in through Felwood's Timbermaw
tunnel (one time — then set your hearth in Nighthaven if you're committing).

- **Catch:** Raw Nightfin Snapper (night), Raw Sunscale Salmon (day),
  Raw Redgill.
- **Estimated:** **7–12 g/h raw, 10–14 g/h cooked.** Slightly below Jademir
  but the safety is absolute and it's day-AND-night viable.

---

### ⛈️ Dossier 5: Bay of Storms — Azshara
**Safety: ★★★☆☆ (contested and gankable in theory; in practice the bay's hidden coves see no traffic) · Convenience: ★★☆☆☆**

The high-roller spot. Azshara's eastern bay holds Stonescale Eel water
**plus a unique prize: Patch of Elemental Water pools**, which yield Essence
of Water — an expensive enchanting/crafting reagent. Pool luck makes this
spot spiky: some hours are average, some hours one pool pays for the session.

- **When:** 12 AM – 12 PM for eels; pools spawn around the clock.
- **Catch:** Stonescale Eel, Large Raw Mightfish, Raw Glossy Mightfish,
  Raw Whitescale Salmon, seasonal squid/bass, elemental water pools.
- **Cooking play:** Whitescale Salmon → **Baked Salmon** (+12/+12, your
  275-skill flex); Glossy Mightfish → Cooked Glossy Mightfish for stamina
  buyers.
- **Estimated:** **10–18 g/h**, highest variance in the guide.
- **Warrior note:** pick a cove, clear the two naga that own it once, and
  they're a non-issue on respawn — you're a 60 warrior, treat them as rage
  generation.

---

### ⚓ Dossier 6: Menethil Harbor — Wetlands
**Safety: ★★★★★ (Alliance port town) · Convenience: ★★★★★**

The lazy classic. Stand on the dock between boat arrivals, AH-adjacent
lifestyle via the mailbox, every amenity in arm's reach. The money is
**Oily Blackmouth** and **Firefin Snapper** — low-skill fish that alchemists
buy forever for Blackmouth Oil and Fire Oil. Clams give Clam Meat for
chowder.

- **When:** anytime. No windows. This is your "watching a movie on the
  second monitor" spot.
- **Estimated:** **4–8 g/h.** Half of Feralas money for one tenth of the
  effort. On PvP-heavy servers, Free Action Potion demand pushes Oily
  Blackmouth up — check your AH.

---

### 🏙️ Dossier 7: The baseline — Stormwind Canals / Ironforge
**Safety: ∞ · Convenience: ∞ · Gold: ~1–2 g/h**

Listed only for honesty: city fishing (Raw Longjaw Mud Snapper, Raw
Brilliant Smallfish) is vendor-trash income. It exists for fishing while
waiting on a raid summon, nothing more. Don't confuse "safest" with "best".

## Chapter 5 — Danger pays: the contested appendix

You asked for safe. These two are *not* safe, but the money is real and a 60
warrior off-peak can pull them off:

### 🐡 Deviate Fish — The Barrens oases (Lushwater Oasis, Forgotten Pools, Stagnant Oasis)
The most valuable common fish in the game, skill requirement 1, and it cooks
into **Savory Deviate Delight** (pirate/ninja transformation — pure novelty,
permanent demand). The catch: it's the middle of Horde territory.
**Off-peak hours only** (server mornings), fish the Forgotten Pools (most
northern, fastest escape toward Ashenvale). The recipe is a rare Barrens
world drop — if you don't own it, **sell raw**; the fish alone moves.
**Estimated: 15–25 g/h raw, more with the recipe** — the best raw g/h in
Classic, priced accordingly in risk.

### 🎣 Stranglethorn Fishing Extravaganza — Sundays 2–4 PM
The weekly tournament: first to 40 **Speckled Tastyfish** wins the
**Arcanite Fishing Pole** (+35, BiS) or other prizes. STV beach on a Sunday
is a PvP bloodbath and you will probably not win your first one — but as a
warrior you can at least fight back, and even non-winners can turn in rare
tournament catches. Go once for the experience; go again if your server's
competition is weak.

## Chapter 6 — The cooking multiplier

Why your 300 Cooking roughly +30–40%'s your income:

| Raw fish | Cooked product | Buff | Skill | Who buys |
|----------|---------------|------|-------|----------|
| Raw Nightfin Snapper + Refreshing Spring Water | **Nightfin Soup** | +8 mana/5 sec, 10 min | 250 | Healers, mages, warlocks — the biggest food market in the game |
| Raw Winter Squid + Soothing Spices | **Grilled Squid** | +10 Agility, 10 min | 240 | Rogues, hunters, warriors |
| Large Raw Mightfish + Hot & Soothing Spices | **Mightfish Steak** | +10 Stam, +10 Spirit | 175 | Tanks, levelers |
| Raw Whitescale Salmon + Soothing Spices | **Baked Salmon** | +12 Stam, +12 Spirit, 15 min | 275 | Tanks |
| Raw Sunscale Salmon | **Poached Sunscale Salmon** | +6 hp/5 sec, 10 min | 250 | Modest — your daytime filler |
| 2× Zesty Clam Meat + Ice Cold Milk + Hot Spices | **Undermine Clam Chowder** | +8 Stam, +8 Spirit | 225 | Budget buyers |

Rules of thumb:
- **Cook between bites.** Drop a campfire, queue 20 soups while the bobber
  does its thing. Cooking time is free time.
- **Never cook Stonescale Eel or Oily Blackmouth.** They're alchemy
  reagents; cooking them destroys their value.
- **Spices logistics:** Soothing Spices and Hot Spices come from cooking
  suppliers — stock 100+ at Feathermoon before heading to remote water.
  Refreshing Spring Water is an innkeeper item; buy stacks.

## Chapter 7 — Reading your server's market

The g/h ranges in this guide swing on three questions you can answer in two
minutes at the AH:

1. **Are guilds progressing through AQ/Naxx-tier content?** Flask of the
   Titans consumption → Stonescale Eel is your #1. Heavy consumable raiding
   → Nightfin Soup demand spikes Tuesday/Wednesday.
2. **Is it a PvP server with active BGs?** Free Action Potions → Oily
   Blackmouth quietly outperforms its tier.
3. **What season is it?** Winter Squid (Sept 23–Mar 20) is scarce and
   expensive in summer — if you stockpiled, summer is when you sell.

Selling craft:
- Post raw fish in **stacks of 20**, buff food in **stacks of 5 and 10**
  (raiders buy per-raid-night quantities).
- Post **Monday evening through Wednesday** — raid reset economics.
- Nightfin Soup and Grilled Squid are *consumed* weekly; this market never
  saturates the way gathering markets do.

## Chapter 8 — A perfect fishing day

The full rotation for a no-lifing Saturday (server time):

| Hours | Where | Doing |
|-------|-------|-------|
| 12 AM – 6 AM | Jademir Lake | **Power hours.** Nightfin window + Stonescale window overlap. Nightfin until bags groan, cooking soup at the fire |
| 6 AM – 12 PM | Feathermoon dock | Stonescale window still open. Eels + Mightfish, mail everything, restock spices and spring water |
| 12 PM – 6 PM | Break, or Menethil | Windows closed — this is movie-fishing time, or AH posting time |
| 6 PM – 12 AM | Lake Kel'Theril or Moonglade | Nightfin window reopens. Soup production continues |

Even the degenerate version of this — just the 12 AM–6 AM block twice a
week — funds a warrior's repair bills, consumables, and a slow march toward
an epic mount.

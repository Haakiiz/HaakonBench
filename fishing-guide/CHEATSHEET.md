# The Ironclad Angler — One-Page Cheat Sheet

Print this. Tape it to the monitor. (Full guide: [README.md](README.md))

## Where do I go *right now*?

```
Is it 6 PM – 6 AM server time?
├─ YES → Jademir Lake, Feralas (Nightfin Snapper → cook Nightfin Soup)
│        Too far / want guards? Lake Kel'Theril (Winterspring) or Moonglade
└─ NO  → Is it before noon?
         ├─ YES → Feathermoon dock, Feralas (Stonescale window open)
         └─ NO  → Menethil Harbor (Oily Blackmouth) or take a break / post AH
```

## The three windows

| Window | Time | Fish |
|--------|------|------|
| 🌙 Nightfin | 6 PM – 6 AM | Raw Nightfin Snapper |
| 🌗 Stonescale | 12 AM – 12 PM | Stonescale Eel (boosted rate) |
| 📅 Season | Sep 23 – Mar 20 | Raw Winter Squid (summer = Raw Summer Bass) |

**Power hours = 12 AM – 6 AM** (both windows open).

## Cook / don't cook

- ✅ **Cook:** Nightfin Snapper → Soup (+8 mp5) · Winter Squid → Grilled
  Squid (+10 Agi) · Large Mightfish → Steak (+10/+10) · Whitescale Salmon →
  Baked Salmon (+12/+12)
- ❌ **Sell RAW:** Stonescale Eel · Oily Blackmouth · Firefin Snapper ·
  Deviate Fish (unless you own the Savory Deviate Delight recipe)

## Shopping list (once)

- Big Iron Fishing Pole · Aquadynamic Fish Attractors · Bright Baubles
- Recipes from **Gikkix** (Steamwheedle Port, Tanaris): Nightfin Soup,
  Grilled Squid, Poached Sunscale Salmon
- Recipes from **Vivianna** (Feathermoon): Mightfish Steak, Baked Salmon
- Stacks of: Refreshing Spring Water, Soothing Spices, Hot Spices

4 A perfect fishing day

The full no-lifing Saturday rotation (server time). Even just the midnight block twice a week funds repairs, consumables, and a slow march to an epic mount.

12 AM – 6 AM
⚡ Power hours — Jademir Lake. Nightfin AND Stonescale windows overlap. Nightfin until your bags groan, cooking soup at the fire between bites.
6 AM – 12 PM
Feathermoon dock. Stonescale window still open — eels + Mightfish. Mail everything, restock spices and Refreshing Spring Water.
12 PM – 6 PM
Windows closed. Menethil movie-fishing (Oily Blackmouth), AH posting, or actually going outside.
6 PM – 12 AM
Nightfin reopens — Lake Kel'Theril or Moonglade. Soup production continues into the night.

6 Reading your server's market

Two minutes at the AH answers everything: guilds pushing AQ/Naxx → Stonescale Eel is king. PvP server with active BGs → Oily Blackmouth quietly outperforms. Post raw fish in stacks of 20, buff food in stacks of 5/10, on Monday–Wednesday — raid reset economics. Soup and squid are consumed weekly; this market never saturates.
